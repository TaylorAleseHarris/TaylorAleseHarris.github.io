Interactions

We offer many exciting interactions for a closer look at some of our animals, including feeding and sometimes touching them!

Beluga Whales!

At our beluga whale interaction, you can feed the whales a variety of fish.
Must be at least 10 years old, accompanied by an adult.
$100/person - includes 15 minute with professional beluga whale caretaker.


Otters!

At our otter interaction, you can feed otters a variety of food including fish, clams, and crabs.
Must be at least 10 years old, accompanied by an adult.
$100/person - includes 15 minutes with professional otter caretaker.


Stingrays!

At our stingray interaction, you can feed and pet them.
Must be at least 6 years old, accompanied by an adult.
$75/person - includes 15 minutes with professional stingray caretaker.
