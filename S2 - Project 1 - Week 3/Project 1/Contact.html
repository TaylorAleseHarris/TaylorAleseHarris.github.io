FAQ's

What colored fungi should I be most cautious of?

Red..

What makes fungi different from other plants?

They reproduce from spores, unlike plants that reproduce from seeds. Also unlike plants, fungi do not produce their own food and have to obtain both food and water from other sources.

Do fungi grow everywhere in the world?

Yes! They grow on all the continents, a few even in Antarctica.
